Package Design

I have designed this phone box as a birthday gift for my sister. Through the design process I took her personality and interests into consideration. She is a Middle Earth fan, so I have chosen that as the theme of the product. She likes minimalistic designs, so I used simple but meaningful symbols from the Lord of the Rings and Hobbit movies. I used gold paint on a black background which made the sybols more vibrant giving an elegant look to the product.
